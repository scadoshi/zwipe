# Video 9 — Star your deck's MVPs

**Theme:** Tell Zwipe which three cards define your deck. They mark the cards
that matter at a glance, and they headline your shared deck page as full art.
**Priority:** P2. Pairs naturally with video #8 (share) — MVPs are what make the
shared page a personality statement. (The suggestion-steering payoff is a later
server phase; do **not** claim it yet — this video is about identifying + showing
the MVPs, which is what's shipped.)
**Hook line:** "Which three cards make this deck yours?"

## Beats (~20s)

| Time | Beat | On-screen caption |
|------|------|-------------------|
| 0:00–0:02 | Deck cards screen, a long grouped list | Every deck has a heart. |
| 0:02–0:05 | Expand a card → tap Star → a gold ★ appears on the row | Star the ones that matter. |
| 0:05–0:09 | Star two more; the ★s stand out in the list | Up to three per deck. |
| 0:09–0:12 | Try a fourth → "This deck already has 3 MVPs" | Three slots. Choose wisely. |
| 0:12–0:15 | Cut to the shared deck page: the MVPs headline it as art | And they headline your shared deck. |
| 0:15–0:16 | Beauty shot of the featured MVP row | The cards that define it. |
| 0:16–0:20 | End card | (video_end_card.png) |

## Shots to capture

1. **Star a card** — expand a card row in the deck cards screen, tap the Star
   button, the warning-gold ★ lands on the compact row.
2. **Three ★s in the list** — with three starred, scroll so multiple ★s are
   visible; they read as "these are the important cards."
3. **The cap** — tap Star on a fourth card → the "This deck already has 3 MVPs"
   message. Quick beat; it communicates the honesty forcing-function (a podium,
   not a wallet).
4. **Share-page payoff** — the same deck's `zwipe.net/deck/…` page, featured
   row: commander + the three MVPs as full art, each labeled. The strongest
   frame; hold it.
