# Video 8 — Share your deck as a link

**Theme:** Every finished deck is one link anyone can open — no account, no
export, and it renders as a clean web page with the commander and MVPs up top.
This is the growth video: the payoff is a link a viewer will actually send.
**Priority:** P1. The share page is the highest-leverage growth surface (a link
works with one user, lands in any MTG space), and "send your deck as a link" is
a clear, enviable hook.
**Hook line:** "Send your whole deck as one link."

## Shots to capture

   MVPs already starred (film video #9's starring first if needed).
2. **Share flow** — More sheet → "Share deck" → the "Share link copied" toast.
   Three-ish taps; rehearse or trim the sheet open in the edit.
3. **Open the link** — best on the *same phone's* browser so the whole take
   stays 9:16 (the page is responsive, single-column on phones). Show the URL
   bar (`zwipe.net/deck/…`) briefly for credibility, then the page render.
4. **Top of page** — the featured row (commander + MVP art, each labeled) and
   the deck tag chips. This is the "wow, it looks like a real product" beat.
5. **Scroll + card art** — scroll the grouped list; on a card, show the art
   preview appearing (hover on desktop capture, or tap-to-expand on mobile).
