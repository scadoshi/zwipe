# Video 10 — Swipe to pick your commander

**Theme:** Choosing a commander is its own swipe deck now — candidates ranked by
what players actually build, a fresh hand each day, and partners that pair
themselves. The "where do I even start" problem, solved by swiping.
**Priority:** P2. Reinforces the core swipe identity on the *first* decision a
new deck makes; the partner auto-pair is a specific, screenshot-worthy delight.
**Hook line:** "Don't know your commander? Swipe for one."

## Shots to capture

1. **Commander swipe** — the Zwipe-select screen, swiping through commander
   candidates. Lead with a few marquee names so the "these are real, good
   commanders" read is instant (popularity base ranks staples first).
2. **Partner autofill** — right-swipe a commander with "Partner with <name>"
   (e.g. a known partner pair). Capture the **"Partner found and selected: …"**
   toast and the partner slot populating automatically. This is the money shot.
3. **Command zone filled** — the deck's command zone now showing both cards,
   then cut back to the normal build flow.
