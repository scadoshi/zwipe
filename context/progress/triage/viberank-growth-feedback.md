# Viberank growth-feedback email

**Source:** unsolicited outreach from "The Viberank Team" (viberank.dev), 2026-06-29.
**Verdict:** undecided — genuinely useful points to think on; the submission/
sponsorship pitch is separate and lower priority.

Outreach with a soft CTA to submit Zwipe to viberank.dev (free submission;
optional paid sponsorship for visibility / SEO articles / security audits /
outreach). Treat the platform pitch with normal skepticism, but several of the
observations are fair and worth acting on independently.

## Useful observations (act on these regardless of Viberank)

**UX**
- Praise: swipe core is well-communicated above the fold; the 13+ theme system
  and three color-blind modes read as strong accessibility/personalization.
- Critique worth weighing: the **ASCII-art logo is likely illegible on small
  mobile screens** and may hurt first-impression clarity for non-dev MTG players.
  (Ties to the already-open "increase Z logo size on zwipe.net" + the deferred
  launcher/app-logo polish in `todo.md`.)

**Marketing / positioning**
- Hero copy ("The Magic: The Gathering deck builder built for mobile") is a
  strong, defensible mobile-first angle vs. desktop-first Moxfield/Archidekt.
- Gaps called out: no social proof (testimonials, user count, press), and no
  SEO-rich long-form content / blog to capture organic search ("best MTG deck
  builder app", "Commander deck builder mobile").

**Conversion**
- Positives: in-browser demo (feel the swipe before install), store badges,
  privacy link.
- Leak called out: **no pricing / free-tier expectations communicated** — users
  don't know if it's free, one-time, subscription, or IAP. (We intentionally
  keep premium open-ended for now; still, a "Free. No ads." line may help.)

**Differentiation (validation, no action needed)**
- Names the moat correctly: mobile-native, gesture-first in a desktop-dominated
  category; synergy-ranked ordering as a real recommendation-engine value-add.

## The platform pitch (decide separately)

- Submit Zwipe at viberank.dev → "Submit your app" (~2 min, free).
- Optional sponsorship unlocks extra visibility / SEO articles / security audits
  / targeted outreach.
- Decision: worth a low-effort free submission? Sponsorship is a spend decision —
  defer until we judge the platform's actual reach. Don't pay before vetting.

## Candidate follow-ups if we pursue any of this

- Add social proof to zwipe.net (live user/usage count — we already expose
  `/api/marketing/stats`; testimonials once we have quotable feedback).
- Communicate the free/no-ads expectation somewhere on the landing page.
- Reconsider the logo legibility on small screens (folds into existing logo work).
- Longer term: a blog / SEO content play for organic search.
